Civic Platforms

Civic platforms provide ways to connect, follow, and engage with your representatives online.

  • GovFaces

    GovFaces connects people and politicians, getting efficiently delivered and meaningful answers to society's biggest questions.

  • Note My Vote

    Note My Vote enables you to vote on UK legislation before it becomes law. Your Member of Parliament will be informed of aggregate opinion in his or her constituency prior to the final vote on legislation in the House of Commons

  • Represent

    Represent is building a modern democracy that helps you have your say. #JoinTheHeard

  • TheyWorkforYou

    TheyWorkForYou lets you find out what your MP, MSP or MLA is doing in your name, read debates, written answers, see what’s coming up in Parliament, and sign up for email alerts when there’s past or future activity on someone or something you’re interested in.

  • WHIP

    Places Parliament in your hands. Providing you with tools to influence your MP and hold them to account.

  • WriteToThem

    Do you know who your MP is? If your answer is ‘no’, you’re not alone – 78% of the UK population cannot name their representative in Parliament. If you need to make contact, that makes it a bit tricky – but WriteToThem solves this problem. Visit the homepage, input your postcode, and it will show you everyone who represents you, from your local councillors up to your MEPs. What’s more, you can then write them a message, simply and quickly.

Open Government

Find the information you need to hold your government to account

  • DataPress

    Custom made for cities, DataPress is a fast and affordable way to transform local government data into resources that your residents can use.

  • Members Interests

    We’ve indexed the House of Commons Register of Members’ Interests so you don’t have to.

Resident Feedback

Report and resolve issues in your local area

  • FixMyStreet

    FixMyStreet makes it easy to report a problem in your area – even if you don’t know who is responsible for fixing it. Reports automatically go to the people who are in charge of fixing them.

  • VoXup

    voXup makes it easy for people to find out what’s being discussed in their neighbourhood, give their opinion and get a response from local representatives like councillors.

Decision Making

Collaborative decision making tools for better decisions

  • Dvel

    Dvel helps you with decisions, snap 2 pics and let your friends help out. Let's dvel!

  • Flotsom

    Seek help with serious and fun decision scenarios, or simply get an opinion with Flotsm, an anonymous social network that taps into the wisdom of the crowd.

  • Loomio

    Loomio is an open-source online tool for collaborative decision-making, built by a team of technologists, activists and social entrepreneurs in Wellington, New Zealand.

    Loomio exists to make it easy for anyone, anywhere, to participate in decisions that affect their lives. It is an online space enabling groups to come together, build shared understanding, arrive at clear outcomes and take action.

  • Whatleads.to

    Whatleads.to helps communities reach consensus on every step towards a big, ambitious goal. Just add what you’re working towards and what leads to it. People can then engage with any idea in a focussed discussion format, or add their own.

Elections

Make an informed decision at the ballot box

  • Election Alerts

    Use MP CV's to read the CV of your candidates, or ask them to make it public so you can decide if you should give them a job.

  • Election Leaflets

    Election Leaflets asks you to photograph the leaflets that get put through your door and share them with the world.

  • Election Mentions

    See what the press has had to say about your constituency and election candidates with Election Mentions. Learn what they’ve said and done, make an informed vote.

  • MeetYourNextMP

    Use MeetYourNextMP.com to find out what events your candidates are going to, so you can talk to them in person, or list events you know they will be attending if we don’t already have them in our database.

  • MP CV's

    Use MP CV's to read the CV of your candidates, or ask them to make it public so you can decide if you should give them a job.

  • PositionDial

    PositionDial is about exploring your political position and seeing who matches: politicians, political parties and friends. We also help you see the different sides of issues: left, right and beyond. Every second of every day, someone is finding their own, unique PositionDial.

  • Verto

    Britain’s first voter advice tool for young people. Verto is Bite The Ballot’s voter advice tool, which aims to help potential voters compare their views and values with the political parties on a variety of issues.

  • Vote for Policies

    Vote for Policies is an independent not-for-profit run by volunteers that helps people to compare manifesto policies without knowing which party they belong to.

  • Vote Power

    Find out how powerful your vote is compared to the UK average.

  • Your Candidates

    Yourcandidates.org.uk is a voter advice site that works on the scale that matters, your local constituency. It provides social media aggregation, predictions of results and comparison of policies for your local candidates for the UK general election in 2015.

  • YourNextMP

    Use YourNextMP.com to find out who your candidates are. If we don’t currently hold all the information in our crowd sourced database then help out by finding a source and adding it to the site.

Privacy

Protect your privacy

  • Docracy: Terms of Service Tracker

    Using Docracy’s unique document change analysis, you can now keep track of terms of service and privacy policies for hundreds of the world’s top sites.

  • HTTPS Everywhere

    HTTPS Everywhere is a Firefox, Chrome, and Opera extension that encrypts your communications with many major websites, making your browsing more secure.

  • GPG tools

    Everything you need to get started with secure communication and encrypting files on OS X in one simple package.

  • Privacy Badger

    Privacy Badger is a browser add-on that stops advertisers and other third-party trackers from secretly tracking where you go and what pages you look at on the web.

  • Tor

    Tor is free software and an open network that helps you defend against traffic analysis, a form of network surveillance that threatens personal freedom and privacy, confidential business activities and relationships, and state security.

  • Tor Browser

    The Tor Browser lets you use Tor on Windows, Mac OS X, or Linux without needing to install any software. It can run off a USB flash drive, comes with a pre-configured web browser to protect your anonymity, and is self-contained.

  • Tutano

    Get your encrypted mailbox for free.
