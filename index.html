Civic Platforms

Civic platforms provide ways to connect, follow, and engage with your representatives online.

  • GovFaces

    GovFaces connects people and politicians.

  • Note My Vote

    Note My Vote enables you to vote on UK legislation before it becomes law.

  • Represent

    Represent is building a modern democracy that helps you have your say.

  • TheyWorkforYou

    TheyWorkForYou lets you find out what your MP, MSP or MLA is doing in your name.

  • WHIP

    Places Parliament in your hands. Providing you with tools to influence your MP and hold them to account.

  • WriteToThem

    Write to your politicians, national or local, for free.

Open Government

Find the information you need to hold your government to account

  • DataPress

    DataPress is a fast and affordable way to transform local government data into resources that residents can use.

  • Members Interests

    Search the House of Commons Register of Members’ Interests

Resident Feedback

Report and resolve issues in your local area

  • FixMyStreet

    FixMyStreet makes it easy to report a problem in your area – even if you don’t know who is responsible for fixing it.

  • VoXup

    voXup makes it easy for people to find out what’s being discussed in their neighbourhood, give their opinion and get a response from local representatives like councillors.

Decision Making

Collaborative decision making tools for better decisions

  • Dvel

    Dvel helps you with decisions, snap 2 pics and let your friends help out. Let's dvel!

  • Flotsom

    An anonymous social network that taps into the wisdom of the crowd.

  • Loomio

    Loomio is an open-source online tool for collaborative decision-making.

  • Whatleads.to

    Helps communities reach consensus on every step towards a big, ambitious goal.

Elections

Make an informed decision at the ballot box

  • Election Alerts

    Read the CV of your candidates, or ask them to make it public so you can decide if you should give them a job.

  • Election Leaflets

    Photograph the leaflets that get put through your door and share them with the world.

  • Election Mentions

    See what the press has had to say about your constituency and election candidates.

  • MeetYourNextMP

    Find out what events your candidates are going to, so you can talk to them in person.

  • MP CV's

    Read the CV of your candidates, or ask them to make it public so you can decide if you should give them a job.

  • PositionDial

    PositionDial is about exploring your political position and seeing who matches: politicians, political parties and friends.

  • Verto

    Britain’s first voter advice tool for young people.

  • Vote for Policies

    Compare manifesto policies without knowing which party they belong to.

  • Vote Power

    Find out how powerful your vote is compared to the UK average.

  • Your Candidates

    A voter advice site that works on the scale that matters, your local constituency.

  • YourNextMP

    Find out who your candidates are.

Privacy

Protect your privacy

  • Docracy: Terms of Service Tracker

    Keep track of terms of service and privacy policies for hundreds of the world’s top sites.

  • HTTPS Everywhere

    Encrypt your communications with many major websites, making your browsing more secure.

  • GPG tools

    Everything you need to get started with secure communications.

  • Privacy Badger

    Stops advertisers and other third-party trackers from secretly tracking where you go and what pages you look at on the web.

  • Tor

    Tor is free software and an open network that helps you defend against surveillance.

  • Tor Browser

    The Tor Browser lets you use Tor on Windows, Mac OS X, or Linux.

  • Tutano

    Get your encrypted mailbox for free.
